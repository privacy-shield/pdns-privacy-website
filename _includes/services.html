The Shield

Why It Matters

Protection

The pDNS Privacy Shield anonymizes data from the outset, ensuring that sensitive information is protected during collection and transmission, preventing traceability to its source and safeguarding both organizations and users.

Real-Time threat Intelligence

By allowing organizations to share anonymized pDNS data, the shield enhances real-time threat intelligence, enabling faster detection and response to cyber threats while maintaining privacy and security.

Open-Source

By making the pDNS Privacy Shield open-source and transparent, organizations can inspect the code, verify its security, and understand how anonymization works while optimizing deployments.
